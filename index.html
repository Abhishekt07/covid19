How it Spreads?

You catch COVID-19

There will no symptoms for first 4-5 days be seen(it can also take upto 14 days), only severe headache, sore throat, loss of taste and smell will be seen initially.

Symptoms are True

Now, you know that you have catched COVID-19. First of all, make it sure, as many people have these symptoms on regular basis. Most serious symptoms are - difficulty breathing or shortness of breath,chest pain or pressure, loss of speech or movement.

Consult your nearest Doctor

Your nearest COVID centre doctor will guide you next. Always call before visiting your doctor or health facility.

Symptoms brief

Most common symptoms : fever,dry cough,tiredness. Less common symptoms : aches and pains,sore throat,diarrhoea,conjunctivitis,headache,loss of taste or smell,a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes.

How to Fight?

1
Step 1 - Maintain Hygiene
Avoid touching your face.
2
Step 2 - Safety First
Maintain at least 1 metre distance between you and people coughing or sneezing.
3
Step 3 - Refine essentials
Wash your hands regularly with soap and water, or clean them with alcohol-based hand rub.
4
Step 4 - Hum Andar, Corona Bahar
Practice physical distancing by avoiding unnecessary travel and staying away from large groups of people.
